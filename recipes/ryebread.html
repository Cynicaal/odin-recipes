Rye bread

Rye bread

Ingidients

  • 700 g white Flour
  • 300 g rye Flour
  • 600 ml Water
  • 35 g Salt
  • 42 g fresh Yeast
  • 10 g Sugar

Procedure

  1. Mix the ingridients together well.
  2. Knead the dough for about 15-20 minutes
  3. Let it rise for 1 hour.
  4. Make two loafs out of risen dough. Put them in the baking plate and let them rise for another 20 minutes
  5. Put the plate in preheated oven (200C) and levae it in for about 50 minutes.
