Pizza Napolitana

Finished pizza Napolitana

Ingidients

  • 1.000 g 00 Flour
  • 650 ml Water
  • 30 g Salt
  • 2 g fresh Yeast
  • 10 g Sugar
  • 25 g Olive Oil

Procedure

  1. Add salt to the water and dissolve it.
  2. Add about 200g of flour to the mixture and stir.
  3. Add yeast and mix it in.
  4. Slowly keep adding the rest of the flour and mix it in thoroughly.
  5. Add sugar and olive oil and keep mixing/kneading.
